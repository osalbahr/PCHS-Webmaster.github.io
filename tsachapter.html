OUR TSA CHAPTER

Panther Creek boasts of a wonderful TSA chapter. Despite the school being relatively young, PCHS has been successful throughout TSA. PCHS has qualified for TSA nationals in many events such as video game design. Recently, the PCHS robotics team has also taken the world by storm with their recent regional championship win and being able to qualify for VEX states for the first time in school history. They have also dominated local competitions as well. PCHS is also doing well in animatronics, engineering, biotechnology, and has added a good Scientific Visualization program and good computer programming classes. Recently, PCHS has added AP Computer Science and AP Computer Science Principles to its plethora of CTE Courses.

This year, the Panther Creek TSA is raising money for the American Cancer Society by selling Krispy Kreme doughnuts. All of the profit made will be donated to ACS.
